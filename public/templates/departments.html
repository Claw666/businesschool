DEPARTMENTS

Department of Accounting

Bringing together internationally renowned academics who research and teach a wide range of topics, with a unique emphasis on corporate governance, taxation, and critical issues in accounting.

Department of Banking and Finance

Researching a range of accounting and finance topics, with a unique emphasis on corporate governance, contemporary accounting issues, computational finance, and financial risk in the banking industry.

Department of Decision Analytics and Risk

Combining excellence in management sciences and decision analysis to work with major international companies and educate future specialists across industry and commerce.

Department of Digital and Data Driven Marketing

Focusing on the challenges of the digital economy to understand the impact of technologies and innovative developments.

Department of HRM and Organisational behaviour

Gaining new insights in the areas of leadership and change through the lens of work-based relationships applied at many levels, including individual, team and organisation.

Department of Strategy Innovation and Entrepreneurship

Providing world-leading expertise on innovation and enterprise with a particular focus on its international dimensions, sustainability, and corporate social responsibility.
